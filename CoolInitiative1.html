Introduction

In these unusual times of limited movement and schools remaining inoperative, it helps to reimagine alternate ways of continuing the teaching learning process. This reimagining, however, needs to be in line with the ground realities of the stakeholders involved. In line with this idea, we are conducting dipstick surveys to understand the ground scenario of teachers, principals and teacher educators, their overall concerns, the challenges they are facing with respect to the teaching learning process and the asks they have for professional development. We are doing a similar survey with student teachers and students of education studies. We plan to share the findings from these surveys on a regular basis so that our, as well as other, interventions geared towards these communities are better informed by and are aligned to the needs of the field.
